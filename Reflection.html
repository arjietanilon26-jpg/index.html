Personal Reflection

In everything I do, I strive to put God First. My choices, actions, and goals are grounded in faith believing that every step I take should honor Him. By prioritizing God above all, I am reminded that true success comes not from my own strength, but from His guidance and grace.

As a student and a digital citizen, I also aim to "Be the Light of the World." Whether online or in person, I choose to act with kindness, respect, and integrity. The digital world is full of negativity and distractions, but I want to use my voice and actions to bring encouragement, positivity, and truth.

Being the light means reflecting God's love and wisdom in my words and decisions. It means being responsible, avoiding harmful content, using social media wisely, and respecting others even when no one is watching. I believe that small acts sharing inspiration, being honest, choosing what is right can shine brightly and inspire others to do the same.

"LET YOUR LIGHT SHINE BEFORE OTHERS."- Matthew 5:16
